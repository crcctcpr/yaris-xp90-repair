## SERVICE INTERVALS

| Item | Interval |
|------|----------|
| Engine oil + filter | 5,000 km / 3 months (severe) OR 10,000 km / 6 months (normal) |
| Air filter | 40,000 km or as needed |
| Cabin filter | 15,000 km |
| Spark plugs (iridium) | 100,000 km |
| Spark plugs (standard) | 30,000 km |
| Timing belt (2NZ-FE ONLY) | **100,000 km / 60,000 miles — CRITICAL** |
| Timing chain (1KR/1NR) | Inspect at 100,000 km |
| Coolant flush | 160,000 km first flush, then every 80,000 km (SLLC) |
| Brake fluid | Every 2 years regardless of mileage |
| Auto trans fluid | 80,000 km (drain/fill), never "lifetime" |
| Manual trans fluid | 40,000 km |

## OBD2 PORT
- **Location:** Under dashboard, driver's side, left of steering column
- **Protocol:** ISO 15765-4 CAN 500kbps (ELM327 ATSP6)
- **ECU address:** 0x7E0 (broadcast 0x7DF)
