# Complete Maintenance Schedule — 2011 Toyota Yaris XP90

> **Normal Driving:** City/suburban driving, moderate speeds, no towing, no extreme temperatures
> **Severe Driving:** Frequent short trips, dusty environments, towing, constant idling, high-speed/high-load driving
> **All intervals in kilometers (km). Convert to months if applicable (1 year ≈ 10,000–15,000 km depending on driving)**

---

## 5,000 KM / 3 MONTHS

### Inspect
- [ ] Tire condition and pressure (check monthly, but first inspection here)
- [ ] Windshield wipers (cracks, streaking)
- [ ] Headlight and brake light function (all bulbs)
- [ ] Undercarriage for obvious leaks or damage (drive through puddle and check)

### Top Up (as needed)
- [ ] Engine oil level (dipstick check)
- [ ] Coolant level (cold engine, reservoir check)
- [ ] Brake fluid level (reservoir, top up only if low)
- [ ] Power steering fluid (if hydraulic PS — most XP90 use EPS so skip)

---

## 10,000 KM / 6 MONTHS — **FIRST OIL CHANGE**

### Change
- [ ] **Engine oil and filter** (5W-30, capacity 3.0L with filter)

### Inspect
- [ ] Tire wear and alignment (if uneven wear observed)
- [ ] Brake pad wear (visual through wheel, or thickness gauge)
- [ ] All hoses (coolant, brake, vacuum) for cracks or leaks
- [ ] Battery terminals for corrosion

### Rotate (Optional but Recommended)
- [ ] Tire rotation (helps even wear; every 10,000 km is ideal)

---

## 15,000 KM / 9 MONTHS

### Change
- [ ] **Cabin air filter** (air quality suffers, especially in dusty regions)

### Inspect
- [ ] Suspension components (bounce test on each corner; excessive bounce = worn struts)
- [ ] Seatbelts for fraying or damage
- [ ] Horn operation

---

## 20,000 KM / 12 MONTHS (1 YEAR)

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter (second change; establish regular schedule)

### Inspect
- [ ] All lights again (especially after winter)
- [ ] Windshield wipers (replace if streaking)
- [ ] All fluid levels (comprehensive check)
- [ ] Brakes under load (test drive with empty parking lot hard braking)

### Rotate
- [ ] Tire rotation (if not done at 10,000 km)

### Service
- [ ] **Wheel alignment check** (if vehicle pulls to one side)

---

## 30,000 KM / 18 MONTHS

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter (third change)
- [ ] **Air filter** (inspect; replace if restricted or dusty)

### Inspect
- [ ] Spark plugs (visual inspection from top — should be light tan/gray, not black or white)
- [ ] Exhaust system (check underneath for rust or holes)
- [ ] Transmission fluid color (dipstick pull) — should be red, not brown

---

## 40,000 KM / 24 MONTHS (2 YEARS)

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter (fourth change)
- [ ] **Brake fluid** (absorbs moisture; prevents corrosion inside brakes)

### Inspect
- [ ] Tire tread depth (minimum 1.6 mm legal limit; recommended 3 mm for safety)
- [ ] Suspension bushings (shake vehicle side-to-side; listen for clunks)
- [ ] Battery (load test if near 2 years old)
- [ ] Cooling system hoses (squeeze for firmness; should not be rock-hard or squishy)

### Service
- [ ] **Wheel alignment check** (especially after any suspension work)

---

## 50,000 KM / 30 MONTHS

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter

### Inspect
- [ ] Brake rotor condition (visual; if scoring deep or thickness <17.0 mm: replace)
- [ ] Steering linkage (grab tie rod ends and shake; should NOT move)

---

## 60,000 KM / 36 MONTHS (3 YEARS)

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter
- [ ] **Manual transmission fluid (if manual)** (75W-90 gear oil change — first or second change)
- [ ] **Cabin air filter** (second change)

### Inspect
- [ ] Brake pad thickness (measure with caliper; minimum 1.0 mm, replace at 2.0 mm)
- [ ] Suspension (test drive over bumps; listen for noise)
- [ ] All lights

### Service
- [ ] **Comprehensive brake inspection:** Check rotor runout, caliper function, brake line condition

---

## 80,000 KM / 48 MONTHS (4 YEARS)

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter
- [ ] **Automatic transmission fluid (if auto)** (partial drain-and-refill with U340E)
- [ ] **Spark plugs** (1KR-FE uses iridium plugs good to 100k km, so inspect closely; may replace)

### Inspect
- [ ] All belts and hoses
- [ ] Transmission for shifting smoothness (road test)
- [ ] Air conditioning function (cool air, no odor)

### Service
- [ ] **Full tire inspection** (rotation, balance, pressure)
- [ ] **Battery load test** (if 4 years old, test CCA rating)

---

## 100,000 KM / 60 MONTHS (5 YEARS)

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter
- [ ] **Coolant flush and refill** (coolant absorbs moisture; inhibitors degrade after 5 years)
- [ ] **Spark plugs** (iridium plugs — may still be good, but condition check recommended; replace at 100k km for peace of mind)
- [ ] **Manual transmission fluid** (second change, if manual)
- [ ] **Cabin air filter** (third change)

### CRITICAL INSPECTION & SERVICE
- [ ] **Timing chain inspection (1KR-FE):** Check for stretch; if P0016 code present, replace chain/tensioner
- [ ] **Timing belt inspection (2NZ-FE):** **REPLACE TIMING BELT and WATER PUMP** (see ENGINE_REPAIR.md) — interference engine, belt failure = catastrophic damage
- [ ] **Brake system overhaul:** Full fluid flush with bleeding, rotor/pad inspection, brake line integrity

### Inspect
- [ ] Engine compression test (verify compression 150–180 psi per cylinder)
- [ ] All suspension components (bushings, struts, ball joints for play/wear)
- [ ] Steering column (tightness, no play in steering wheel)
- [ ] Exhaust system complete inspection

### Service
- [ ] **Full wheel alignment** (especially if any suspension work done)
- [ ] **Air conditioning system check** (pressure test, refrigerant level)
- [ ] **Battery replacement** (most batteries fail between 3–5 years)

---

## 120,000 KM / 72 MONTHS (6 YEARS)

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter
- [ ] **Brake fluid** (second flush; every 2 years is ideal)

### Inspect
- [ ] Suspension for component wear/cracks
- [ ] All electrical connectors (corrosion, especially around engine bay)

---

## 140,000 KM / 84 MONTHS

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter

### Inspect
- [ ] Engine bay hoses thoroughly (coolant, brake, PCV) for age cracks
- [ ] Transmission fluid color (both manual and auto)

---

## 160,000 KM / 96 MONTHS (8 YEARS)

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter
- [ ] **Manual transmission fluid** (third change, if manual)
- [ ] **Brake fluid** (third flush)
- [ ] **Cabin air filter**

### Inspect
- [ ] Entire drivetrain (undercarriage inspection for leaks/damage)
- [ ] Interior for water intrusion (check carpet under seats for damp)

### Service
- [ ] **Suspension inspection:** Struts may be nearing end of life; test for leaking oil or worn seals
- [ ] **Brake caliper function:** Test for stickiness or excessive play

---

## 180,000 KM / 108 MONTHS

### Change
- [ ] Engine oil and filter
- [ ] **Automatic transmission fluid** (second full flush, if auto)

### Inspect
- [ ] Engine for oil seeps (gasket condition)
- [ ] All electrical systems (alternator output, starter engagement)

### Service
- [ ] **Full electrical diagnostic** (battery, alternator, starter, all lights)

---

## 200,000 KM / 120 MONTHS (10 YEARS)

### Major Service Recommendations
- [ ] **Engine compression test** (second comprehensive test; compare to 100k km baseline)
- [ ] **Suspension overhaul:** Struts are likely worn; consider replacing front and rear
- [ ] **Brake system complete overhaul:** Calipers may need rebuild/replacement; brake lines may corrode
- [ ] **Transmission fluid full flush** (for both manual and auto)
- [ ] **Engine hoses full replacement** (rubber hardens/cracks after 10 years)
- [ ] **Battery replacement** (even if still starting, reliability decreases after 5–6 years)
- [ ] **All seals inspection:** Valve cover, pan gasket, transmission seals for weeping

### Inspect
- [ ] VVT-i solenoid and system (P1349 codes common at this mileage due to sludge)
- [ ] Catalytic converter efficiency (run emissions test if available)
- [ ] All suspension bushings (clunking will be obvious by this point)

### Optional Overhauls (Vehicle-Dependent)
- [ ] **Transmission overhaul** (if showing signs of slipping, hard shifts, or codes)
- [ ] **Engine rebuild** (if compression is low or oil consumption is high)
- [ ] **Cooling system inspection:** Radiator may need replacement

## SEVERITY NOTES

### For SEVERE Driving (Dusty, Short Trips, Towing, Hot Climates)
| Item | Normal Interval | Severe Interval |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Oil change | 10,000 km | 5,000 km |
| Air filter | 30,000 km | 15,000 km |
| Cabin air filter | 15,000–20,000 km | 10,000 km |
| Transmission fluid (auto) | 80,000–100,000 km | 40,000–50,000 km |
| Brake inspection | 60,000 km | 30,000 km |
| Cooling system flush | 100,000 km / 5 years | 50,000 km / 2–3 years |

### For Cold Climates
- **Battery:** Test annually in fall; replace every 3–4 years instead of 5
- **Coolant:** Verify 50/50 mix is correct for lowest expected temperature (check freeze point)
- **Tire pressure:** Check monthly; cold reduces pressure by ~5% per 10°C temperature drop
- **Engine block heater:** Consider installing for sub-zero climates (aids cold start)

### For Hot Climates
- **Coolant:** Verify boiling point is adequate; consider higher-concentration SLLC
- **Transmission fluid (auto):** Change more frequently (40,000 km intervals) due to heat degradation
- **Brake fluid:** Change every 12–18 months instead of 24 months (moisture absorption accelerates in humidity)
- **Battery:** Reduce expected lifespan by 1–2 years

## RECORD-KEEPING

Maintain a service log for:
- Oil and fluid changes (date, km, fluid type, quantity)
- Major repairs (parts replaced, labor, date)
- Inspection findings (conditions observed, recommendations for future)
- Parts warranty dates (to reference if premature failure)

This log is valuable for:
- Resale value documentation
- Warranty claim support
- Identifying patterns in component failures
- Planning future maintenance
