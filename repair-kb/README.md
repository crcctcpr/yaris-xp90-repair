## ⚠️ Critical Safety Notes

1. **Timing belt (2NZ-FE):** Interference engine — belt failure causes $1,500–2,500 damage. Replace every 60,000 km without exception.
2. **Airbag work:** Always disconnect battery and wait 90 seconds before removing steering wheel or SRS components.
3. **Brake work:** Never reuse old fluid; flush and refill with fresh DOT 3 to prevent brake failure.
4. **Refrigerant handling:** R134a is EPA-regulated; requires certification to purchase/handle in USA.
5. **Clutch breaking in:** Light driving for 50 km after replacement; no hard acceleration or towing during break-in.
